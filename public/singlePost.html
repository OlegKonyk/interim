Lyft

Get a ride in your city

New age

Those days are gone when you were used to stand on a street corner and pushing out your hand to get the attention of almost every cab, now for many city tenants, finding a ride is as easy as pulling out your phone, tapping in an app, and waiting for a town car to pick you up.
There is only one amazing ride-sharing app that leads the scene and the name of that greatest beauty is Lyft. Lyft was launched in 2012 and this app is available for one of the finest operating systems, Android and iOS.

Simply beautifull

Lyft is very well known for its simplicity, all you have to do is just to install the application in your mobile phone and signup. After the signup you will be asked to provide a valid phone number and then you have to provide a proper link for the payment like Google wallet or PayPal. Lyft is considered to be the best service in entire California because Lyft allows its customers to update their profile; customers can add their hometowns addresses and their music preferences etc. All this valuable information helps the driver to engage the customers more effectively.
All drivers at Lyft are very well mannered and extremely well trained from the best driving schools, due to this reason Lyft offers you the guarantee of the safe rides. No other service in the whole America can claim such incredible assurance.
Whenever you need a ride just open the Lyft app and follow the on-screen prompts because they will further help you. With the help of app, you will be able see a map of your present location and also this app will visualize available cars close by. Once you confirmed a request for the ride and enter your desired location, the app will immediately contact nearby available drivers.
After all this process now driver will accept the request and then you can visit the driver’s profile for example name of the driver, color of the car, model of the car and past reviews about the driver. At Lyft the only concern which is considered important is that client should be entertained with the very best of the services at very cheap rates. To offer the paramount services, Lyft offers a coupon of $20 for those who are first riders of Lyft.
Lyft takes every possible safety measures to monitor their drivers and promote safety. Lyft is wonderfully polite and kind to their drivers. Lyft app offers passengers to add a tip to the driver’s fare if customer is happy with the driver. If a driver rates a Lyft passenger three stars or less than three, you will not receive any more requests from the same passenger again. Do not forget about the most important point, Lyft presents the renowned Power Driver Bonuses. If you complete a definite number of rides per week, you will get to keep a bigger percentage of your fare because Lyft takes just a smaller cut.
So if you are in pursuit of superlative and safe services, then you are warmly welcome to join the Lyft family.
